July 7th, 2014
Southwest Chief at San Bernardino
This is the train I took when moving from Boston to Redlands in 2012. Having running daily along the same route for 40 plus years, the Chief is a legacy train across generations. This train was heading to the east, carrying hundreds of people who filled with summer fun. Wish I could be one of the passengers, taking all my belongs along, moving to a new place after dozens of hours, and starting a brand new life...
